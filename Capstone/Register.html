Why train Brazilian Jiu Jitsu?

  • It's a great workout. You use your entire body...things will hurt that you didn't know could hurt.
  • It's a fun family activity. Your family stays in shape, and everyone learns self defense.
  • You become resilient. BJJ teaches that smaller, weaker individuals can overcome a larger, stronger opponent. This takes time, perseverance, and discipline.
  • It builds confidence. As you learn more about technique, the lever, and the base, you gain knowledge of self-defense and protection. Your attitude and outlook completely change when you have the tools to defend yourself, or potentially others.
  • It teaches patience. It takes several years to become competent in this art. Consistency and discipline are tools you will yield as you continue your journey of personal growth.
  • It's addictive (in a good way). The dopamine flood after this kind of workout is something your body seeks more of.
